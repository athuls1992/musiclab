01.Music Experience

Music moved on from being a small part of my life to a passion. So when I stopped practicing music there was a hole. That’s when the transition happened . I went back to music with an extra vigor. Soon I discovered my fondness for the instruments (Piano) Along with my stage performance I was parallel teaching music in Music Academies/ Schools etc, This helped me to transition my music knowledge & skills to students & budding musicians.

02. Studies

  • I am certified in Western Vocals & Piano from ABRSM (Associate Board of Royal School Of Music & Trinity College, London
  • With a teaching experience of over 10+years

To me, music is more than just sound, it’s an art with rhythm , tune, emotion, and meaning. It can convey feelings of patriotism, sorrow, happiness, inspiration, or spirituality that can stir up emotions, and bring about change. This, I believe is the best form of music and the reason for its revolution over the ages as a mean of communication.

03. Did you know?

The psychology faculty of the BUAP University in Mexico created a study that determined people with anxiety who listen to heavy metal end up more anxious than before. However, our brains can also be positively effects music has on people is that it regulates stress hormones, makes our memories stronger, and it effects our pulse and heartbeat. Research also proves stating, more brain stimulation comes from musical rhythms than anything else, which is most likely why so many people listen to music while, studying, house work, or while practicing their sports, outdoor tasks etc.
