About us

Musik Lab is an on-line and offline music education platform that makes learning music fun and accessible to people of all ages.
We trained 200+ people of the age group between 4 years to 85 years which included -

  • Children
  • Expecting Mothers
  • Senior Citizens
  • Doctors
  • Pilot
  • Bank Employees etc

Musik Lab was established in 2019, with the aim of creating diverse learning environment for students & adults to learn music skills.
We ensured all their interests were considered while preparing customized music syllabus which helped us understand not just their passion but their challenges too. This deep dive analysis helped me in providing complete focus, time & curriculum as per each individual’s choice & preferences.

We at Musik Lab, encourage creativity, by involving most experienced and talented faculty and updated curriculum in affiliation with the Trinity College, London.
